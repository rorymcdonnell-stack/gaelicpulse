The Brendan Martin

The Brendan Martin Cup is the senior All-Ireland Ladies Football Championship. Senior counties play provincial championships first, then enter an All-Ireland group stage, and finally a knockout round of quarter-finals through the final at Croke Park. The Intermediate (Mary Quinn) and Junior (West County Hotel) championships run on parallel tracks.
